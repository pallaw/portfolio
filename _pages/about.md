# About Me

I'm **Pallaw Pathak**, a Senior Android Engineer with over 10 years of experience in mobile development. I specialize in Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP) and Compose Multiplatform, building clean, scalable architectures and driving cross-platform innovation.
